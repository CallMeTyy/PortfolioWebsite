SpaceRace


General Information

Summary: SpaceRace is a chaotic versus platforming game in which you race to the end. Shoot each other, lock each other out or walk fast enough to kill players outside of the screen!
Coding Platform: GXP Engine (C# .NET)
Target Platform: PC/Arcade
Development Time: 2 Weeks (2021)
Team (6 people):
- Artists: Tycho van Bussel, Mona Wu and Vy Nguyen
- Designers: Andrew Petrila and Teun de Vries
- Engineer: Tycho Tuitert

Description & Contributions

SpaceRace is a game made for arcades. It's a chaotic platformer made in GXP, using their very primitive collision handler.

My Contributions
During this project I worked as the programmer and group (&scrum) leader. My main learning points of the project were:
- Leading a group of people to project completion
- Creating a simple platformer using primitive collisions (AA)
- Rocket & Puzzle door physics
- Camera controls & player following
- Player controllers (max 2 at once)
- Audio, UI & Art integration
- Tiled integration (for level creation)
