SPACE

Space Pirate Alpacas doing a Competition Event


General Information

Summary: SPACE is a chaotic local party game where players connect their phone to the host pc and compete to be the best kitten! There are 6 small minigames that use the different sensors of the phone, so blow, shake, scratch or turn your way to victory!
Coding Platform: Unity
Target Platform: PC/Mobile
Development Time: 3 Weeks
Team (7 people):
- Artists: Bram Vaarwerk, Kevin Nijkamp and Vy Nguyen
- Designers: Ira Evers, Jan de Weijer and Teun de Vries
- Engineer: Tycho Tuitert

Description & Contributions

SPACE is a group project where we were tasked with the creation of a party game. The game uses OSC networking to send data back & forth from the host pc and the connected phones. 6 minigames fill up the game, consisting off:
- Frenzy Fires: Extinguis the flames as quickly as possible by blowing into the microphone!
- Lazer Lightup: Fight a 2v2 battle with lazers! Shake your phone to power up the laser!
- Wasting Water: Close the valves as quickly as possible by rotating your phone!
- Lava Landing: Try to stay out of the lava while pushing your friends from falling platforms!
- Beach Bounce: Dodge the waves and jump over them by shaking your phone!
- Window Washing: Wash the windows using the touch screen on your phone!
